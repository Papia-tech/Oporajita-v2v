About Birangana

Birangana is an AI-powered women's safety platform designed to provide emergency assistance, verified resources, anonymous community storytelling, and safe navigation. Our mission is to empower every woman with confidence, security, and technology.

Who We Are

Birangana is a dedicated women's safety platform that combines technology, awareness, community support, and emergency assistance to help women feel safe, confident, and empowered everywhere.

Our Mission

To provide women with reliable safety tools, verified resources, emergency support, and a secure platform where they can seek help, share experiences, and build confidence.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every woman feels safe, respected, independent, and empowered through innovative technology and compassionate communities.

Our Goal

Our goal is to reduce fear, encourage awareness, strengthen community support, and make safety accessible to every woman through digital innovation.

Our Core Values

Everything we build is based on trust, respect, safety, and empowerment.

Respect

Every woman deserves dignity, equality, and respect regardless of age, profession, or background.

Safety

We prioritize safety by providing emergency support, resources, and awareness tools that can make a real difference.

Community

Building a supportive community where women help, inspire, and protect each other.

Innovation

Using modern technology and AI solutions to improve women's safety and confidence.
